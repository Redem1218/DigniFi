Section 03 — The Opportunity

Two ways to win — immediately

The opportunity isn't theoretical. It lives inside the dealer network DAS already has. There are two distinct levers.

Lever 1 — Close the Funnel Gap
$3.9M
Opportunity: existing 142 dealers, approved consumers who didn't fund.

1,702 consumers were already approved. They didn't need more credit decisioning — they needed someone to follow up. With DAS's engagement infrastructure and DigniFi's re-engagement tools, converting even half of these approved applications would add ~$2M in GMV annually from the same footprint.
Lever 2 — Activate the Full DAS Network
$173M+
Opportunity: DAS's broader dealer network at current per-dealer performance.

142 in-common dealers generated $34,700 in average annual GMV each. DAS works with 5,000+ franchise and independent dealers. Applying our proven per-dealer GMV rate across just 10% of the DAS network (500 dealers) would generate $17.3M in annual GMV — with full activation approaching $173M+.
The math on doing nothing is expensive

Every month the current funnel runs at a 21% funded rate, dealers are leaving proven revenue behind. These aren't cold prospects — they're customers who walked in, asked for financing, and got approved. The only thing missing is a coordinated effort to get them across the finish line.

DigniFi + DAS have a unique structural advantage: DAS owns the service lane relationship. DigniFi owns the financing infrastructure. Together, we close the loop.
